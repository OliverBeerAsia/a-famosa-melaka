# Nano Banana Asset Creation Briefs

## A Famosa: Streets of Golden Melaka

**Art Style Target:** Ultima 8: Pagan / Ultima VII
**Resolution:** 16x16 base tiles, 24x48 characters (scaled)
**Palette:** Warm tropical tones, Portuguese colonial colors, VGA-era richness

---

## 🗺️ MAP/ENVIRONMENT BRIEFS

### 1. A FAMOSA FORTRESS GATE
**Atmosphere:** Imposing, colonial power, Portuguese military might

**Visual Elements:**
- Massive stone gateway with Portuguese coat of arms
- Morion-helmeted guards standing at attention
- Weathered limestone walls with moss patches
- Wooden drawbridge mechanism (decorative)
- Red and gold Portuguese banners hanging from walls
- Cannon emplacements on the ramparts
- Iron-barred wooden doors
- Cobblestone courtyard with drainage channels
- Guard tower with lookout platform
- Tropical vines creeping up ancient stonework

**Color Palette:**
- Gray/blue limestone (#4A4A5A to #7A7A8A)
- Warm sandstone highlights (#8A8575)
- Deep red banners (#8B0000)
- Gold trim (#D4AF37)
- Tropical green vines (#2A5525)

**Mood:** Humid afternoon, guards sweating in armor, the weight of empire

---

### 2. RUA DIREITA (MAIN STREET)
**Atmosphere:** Bustling, crowded, the smell of spices and commerce

**Visual Elements:**
- Narrow cobblestone street winding between buildings
- Portuguese whitewashed buildings with terracotta roofs
- Colorful market awnings (striped red/white, blue/yellow)
- Overflowing market stalls with:
  - Piles of golden turmeric
  - Saffron in ceramic bowls
  - Pepper in burlap sacks
  - Cinnamon bark bundles
  - Cloves in woven baskets
- Chinese porcelain displayed on wooden tables
- Silk fabrics hanging from lines
- Portuguese tavern with wooden sign (tankard symbol)
- Money changers with scales and coin piles
- Caged exotic birds (parrots, songbirds)
- Hanging lanterns (unlit by day, ready for evening)
- Horse-drawn cart loaded with cargo
- Beggars in doorways
- Children playing with hoops

**Color Palette:**
- Whitewash walls (#F5F0E5)
- Terracotta roofs (#AA5020 to #DA7A40)
- Colorful awnings (varied)
- Golden spices (#DAA540)
- Rich fabric colors
- Warm cobblestones (#6A6560)

**Mood:** Sensory overload, babel of languages, tropical heat

### 4. THE WATERFRONT QUAY
**Atmosphere:** Maritime bustle, international trade, adventure departing

  - Small fishing boats
- Cargo being loaded/unloaded:
  - Ceramic amphoras

  - Rolls of silk
  - Spice barrels
- Rope coils everywhere
- Anchor chains
- Sailors of all nations milling about
- Harbor master's counting house
- Fishermen mending nets
- Seagulls on posts
- Water lapping against pilings
- Warehouses with open doors showing goods

**Color Palette:**
- Weathered dock wood (#5A3B20 to #7A5130)
- Deep harbor water (#1A3555 to #3A5A6A)
- White sail canvas (#E8DCC8)
- Rope tan (#7A7540)
- Ship hulls (varied browns, some with color)

**Mood:** Salt air, creaking wood, the call of distant shores

**Color Palette:**
- Bamboo/thatch (#7A6035 to #9A8555)

**Mood:** Peaceful, traditional, life continuing despite colonizers

## 📐 TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Tile Size
- Ground tiles: 16x16 pixels
- Wall tiles: 16x32 pixels (for height)
- Large objects: 32x32 or 32x64

### Character Size
- Width: 16-24 pixels
- Height: 32-48 pixels
- Animation: 4 frames per direction minimum

### Color Depth
- 8 shades per material minimum
- Use dithering for gradients
- Limited palette per asset (32-48 colors)

### File Format
- PNG with transparency
- Sprite sheets for animations
- Individual files for static objects

### Naming Convention
```
tiles/ground-cobblestone.png
tiles/wall-whitewash.png
characters/player-sheet.png
characters/fernao-gomes.png
objects/market-stall.png
objects/spice-pile-turmeric.png
```
